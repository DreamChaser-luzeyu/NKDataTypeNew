(b)
Let G = (V,E) be any n vertex strongly connected digraph. Since G contains a directed path from i to j and from j to i for every pair of vertices i and j, diin >= 1 and diout >= 1, 1 <= i <= n. Hence, the sum of the in-degrees and out-degrees of all vertices is >= 2n. If |E| = e, then the sum of the in-degrees and out-degrees is 2e (Property 2). So, 2e >= 2n or e >= n.
